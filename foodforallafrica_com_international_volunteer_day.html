International Volunteer Day

On this 2024 International Volunteer Day for Economic and Social Development, we celebrate the heart and dedication of our volunteers.

“Volunteers do not necessarily have the time; they just have the heart.” At Food For All Africa, volunteers are the bedrock of everything we do. Their passion, selflessness, and commitment to a better future help us create a foundation of change that reaches far and wide.

Whether it’s through a few hours or long-term service, volunteers are making a huge difference in the fight against food waste, hunger, and poverty. Together, we’re building a Ghana where no one goes hungry.

“No one is more cherished in this world than someone who lightens the burden of another.” Thank you for your time, your heart, and your service.
